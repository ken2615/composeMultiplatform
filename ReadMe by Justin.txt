This is the Signup form that will be part of the Exercise App. This page will get the User details including weight and height. With the given values on the weight and height, the app will calculate the User's Body Mass Index(BMI) and give the recommended exercises for the User.
The bottom bar have navigations through the app(Home, profile, settings..). The top bar have the name of the page and a drawer that will have links to specific exercises.
